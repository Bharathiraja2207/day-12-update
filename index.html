SUBRAMANIA BHARATHI

The National Poet of india

Rebel poet

C. Subramania Bharathi was a Tamil writer, poet, journalist, Indian independence activist, social reformer and polyglot.

He was bestowed the title "Bharathi" for his excellence in poetry. He was a pioneer of modern Tamil poetry and is considered one of the greatest Tamil literary figures of all time.


Achevments

Three of his greatest works namely, Kuyil Pattu, Panchali Sapatham and Kannan Pattu were composed during 1912. He also translated Vedic hymns, Patanjali's Yoga Sutra and Bhagavat Gita to Tamil. Bharathi entered India near Cuddalore in November 1918 and was promptly arrested.
